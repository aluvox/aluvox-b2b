# TT-001 — Titanium Luggage on Wheels

## Product Overview

The Aluvox TT-001 is a titanium hard-shell carry-on suitcase designed for the ultra-premium luggage segment. It represents Aluvox's highest material specification — aerospace-grade titanium alloy construction with in-house forming at our Dongguan facility.

## Specifications

| Feature | Specification |
|---|---|
| Shell material | Aerospace-grade titanium alloy |
| Construction | Full titanium shell |
| Wheels | Four-wheel 360° spinner |
| Handle | Telescopic handle |
| Lock | TSA-approved |
| Format | Carry-on |
| Surface finish | Material-native titanium finish |

## Why Titanium

- Lighter than aluminum at equivalent structural performance
- Stronger than steel with superior impact resistance
- Corrosion resistant without requiring surface treatment
- Distinctive surface character that cannot be replicated with paint or coating on other materials
- Ultra-premium brand positioning for retail price points of $400+

## Brand Positioning

The TT-001 is designed as a hero SKU — a flagship product that establishes material authority at the top of a brand's product line. Most brands use titanium alongside an aluminum or PC core range rather than building an entire line around the material.
