# Aluvox — Premium Hard-Shell Luggage Manufacturer Since 1995

Aluvox is a luggage OEM/ODM manufacturer based in Dongguan, Guangdong, China. We operate a 49,600 m² production facility with 10 dedicated production lines and a 250-person specialist team covering R&D, tooling, production, and quality control.

## What We Manufacture

| Product Line | Materials | MOQ |
|---|---|---|
| Hard-shell luggage | Aluminum (Series 5), PC, ABS, PP | 300 pcs |
| Titanium luggage | Aerospace-grade titanium alloy | 300–500 pcs |
| Professional makeup cases | Aluminum, ABS, PVC, Nylon | 300 pcs |
| Travel bags | Various | 300 pcs |

## Production Capabilities

- In-house aluminum stamping with precision steel dies
- In-house anodizing, brushing, and sandblasting surface treatment
- PC/ABS vacuum thermoforming and injection molding
- Complete hardware assembly (wheels, handles, locks, zippers)
- In-house QC laboratory covering 7 pre-shipment test categories
- ISO 10012 measurement management system certification
- Multiple utility model and design patents
